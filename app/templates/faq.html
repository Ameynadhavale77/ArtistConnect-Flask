How do I get verified on ArtistConnect?

Upload clear profile photos, add demo videos/links, fill out your complete profile, and provide valid contact information. Our team reviews profiles within 2-3 business days.

When do I get paid for my performances?

Payment is released 24 hours after your successful performance. You'll receive payment via bank transfer or UPI as per your preference.

Cancellations within 24 hours require 50% payment. Within 6 hours require full payment. We protect artists from last-minute cancellations.

Can I set my own rates?

Yes! You have full control over your pricing. Set different rates for different types of events, and adjust based on demand in your area.

No hidden fees! You pay the artist's rate + 10% service fee. Everything is transparent upfront. No surprise charges ever.
